Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website to improve people’s experiences at Christmas time. The page will have tabs with Christmas movie suggestions, activities to do at Christmas time, recipes, and decor ideas. This website will help relieve some of the pressure that comes with planning fun Christmas activities by providing ideas and inspiration.

Audience

The intended audience for this site will be anyone looking for fun group activities to do around the holiday season. This could include a mother looking for activities to do with her kids or a college student looking for party recipes and decor inspiration.

Content

Home page

Christmas is a time for joy and a time to make memories with those you love. This site provides ideas and suggestions for holiday activities to bring you and your family joy. It provides a list of movie recommendations and suggestions for traditions to start doing as a family to serve others and to bring you closer together.

Images for the Home page

Traditions

Going to see Christmas Lights – This is an inexpensive and fun family activity that can be done with people of all ages. Families can appreciate the joy of the Christmas season as they look at the lights either on houses around their neighborhood or lights put up on trees in their community. This activity can either be done on its own, or a group could treat themselves to hot chocolate afterwards to warm up from the cold. Baking/Decorating Christmas cookies – This is a fun activity that can be done in the comfort of your own home. The whole group gets to work together to bake a delicious product. After making and shaping the cookies, the whole group gets to show off their artistic abilities by decorating the cookies. The cookies can either all be eaten by the group, or this activity could become a service activity if the group decides to drop off cookies to their friends, neighbors, or family. Secret Santa Gift Exchange – This is a fun activity where each person gets the name of another person in the group. Then, their responsibility is to buy an inexpensive gift for that person but to keep it a secret. Everyone in the group then gets together and gives each other their gift closer to Christmas time. Decorating the Christmas Tree – This family activity gets everybody in the holiday spirit and helps everyone to have a fun time preparing your home for Christmas. It’s an activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Christmas Caroling – This is a great way to bring the Christmas spirit to the group and to spread Christmas cheer. Caroling is another inexpensive and fun activity that can be done outdoors. It can also be done as service if the group wants to sing to a group who might need some extra happiness during the Christmas season. For example, singing to a group at a care center or perhaps a neighbor who might be lonely could be a great way to bring joy to them and to those they sing to.

Movie Recommendations

White Christmas – A wholesome family musical that will get the whole family in the mood to go skiing at a cozy cabin. The Grinch – The Grinch is a fun family cartoon that focuses on the concept of change. The Grinch learns the true meaning of Christmas and realizes that happiness comes from helping others. Home Alone – Home Alone is a movie that focuses on character growth and the importance of families. It’s a fun holiday comedy that will get the kids excited for Christmas. Elf – Elf is a movie with comedic character growth and that teaches the true meaning of family. Buddy the Elf helps his long-lost father to learn what it really means to be humble and take care of your family. A Charlie Brown Christmas – A holiday classic, A Charlie Brown Christmas teaches its audience that Christmas is more than the superficial celebrations that are so often focused on. Charlie Brown and his friends learn the true meaning of Christmas. It’s a Wonderful Life – This movie teaches about what things truly matter in life. While hardship and financial struggles may arise, focusing on the good things, especially family, will help us all to become happier.
