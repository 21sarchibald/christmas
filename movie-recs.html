Movie Recommendations

White Christmas

A wholesome family musical that will get the whole family in the mood to go skiing at a cozy cabin.

The Grinch

The Grinch is a fun family cartoon that focuses on the concept of change. The Grinch learns the true meaning of Christmas and realizes that happiness comes from helping others.

Home Alone

Home Alone is a movie that focuses on character growth and the importance of families. Itâ€™s a fun holiday comedy that will get the kids excited for Christmas.

Elf

Elf is a movie with comedic character growth and that teaches the true meaning of family. Buddy the Elf helps his long-lost father to learn what it really means to be humble and take care of your family.

A Charlie Brown Christmas

A holiday classic, A Charlie Brown Christmas teaches its audience that Christmas is more than the superficial celebrations that are so often focused on. Charlie Brown and his friends learn the true meaning of Christmas.

It's A Wonderful Life

This movie teaches about what things truly matter in life. While hardship and financial struggles may arise, focusing on the good things, especially family, will help us all to become happier.
